25 Years of Work and Still Going Strong

Since 1998, the East Bali Poverty Project has been empowering communities in the Ban district through education, collaboration, and sustainable initiatives. With a focus on ownership and self-reliance, their mission is to eradicate poverty and develop community leaders in Bali’s poorest region. Learn more about their impactful work on the ground.

About Us

East Bali Poverty Project (EBPP) is a non-profit organisation established in 1998 by a British resident of Bali after an appeal for help by an isolated rural village in the mountains of East Bali, forgotten by time and progress.
