HARDIKNAS (National Education Day) Celebrations – 2 May, 2025



An emotional and happy atmosphere enveloped the students of EBPP High School at the end of April. A total of 29 grade XII students officially completed their education after taking the final semester exams from April 25-30, 2025. This exam was the end of their long journey studying at a school located in a remote village, full of enthusiasm and challenges.

For most students, this graduation is not just about completing school, but it also marks success in overcoming a variety of limitations, from difficult road access to limited learning facilities. However, the unyielding spirit and support from teachers and families brought them to this historic moment.

Tears of emotion and big smiles coloured the faces of these students as they thanked the teachers who had accompanied them along their 12-year journey from the beginning. Many of them now dream of continuing their education to a higher level, some will take part in training to work abroad; and all will become an inspiration for their younger siblings.

This graduation is clear proof that with the right opportunity, even children from disadvantaged families in remote areas can achieve a bright future. Congratulations to the 29 students who graduated from EBPP High School! Your life journey has just begun.

By, Ponirin, EBPP Mathematics teacher
