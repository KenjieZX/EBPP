2025 Priority Projects & Fundraising Targets

1. Support 81 Manikai children's continued education

Our 81 students in Manikai remote EBPP school on Mount Abang rely on EBPP for their education, from age 6 to 18, due to geographic and social issues being too far and no reliable transport available in this large East Bali mountain village. Most parents are illiterate and see their children’s education as their only future and to escape their cycle of poverty. It only costs AU$11/student/year – enabled costs US$33,337.

2. Malnutrition outreach and intervention

Developing a replicable “family model” to eliminate malnutrition ensuring that 150 families living in the 15 Ban villages of Ban village through a comprehensive 5-year interactive malnutrition study (2023 is Year 4). In partnership with Gede Foundation, our team of trained young local pregnant women as peer families; families with babies under 2 years; and adolescent girls will receive regular home visits and peer led advice. We have so far raised US$23,893 (AU$35,900); funds still needed for 2023 is US$18,406 (AU$27,600).

Learn More

3. Improve dirt road infrastructure for 700 families & 3 schools

Improve unsafe dirt roads for safer access for our students to 3 EBPP Manikaji, Darmaji & Jatituhu schools, and for local communities for safe access to local and regional health facilities and enable improved farming outputs and economic development by improving transport of farming crops to local and regional markets.

  • Repair 3.7km dirt road from Manikai School to Darmaji School: US$19,275
  • Repair 2.8km dirt road from Darmaji School to Jatituhu School: US$8,245
